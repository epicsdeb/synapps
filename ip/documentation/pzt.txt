
Queensgate AX301 piezo controller
---------------------------------

- EPICS (serial) support consists of a database (pzt.db) and a 
  display (pzt.adl). 

- The serial port connections are as follows:
   
  db9(ioc)    db25(piezo controller)
  --------    ----------------------
     2          2 
     3          3 
     5          7
